Chronology

The story develops gradually, from local races to the creation of the professional Project D team

  1. First Stage (1998) - introduction to the hero and first victories on Mt. Akina
  2. Second Stage (1999) - the appearance of the Emperor team and the AWD Lancer Evo
  3. Third Stage (2001) - a feature film, winter racing and graduation
  4. Fourth Stage (2004) - creation of Project D and conquest of other prefectures
  5. Fifth Stage (2012) - final battles in Kanagawa Prefecture
  6. Final Stage (2014) - the epic conclusion to the story
